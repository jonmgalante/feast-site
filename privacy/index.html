Feast Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Feast is designed to help you save and organize places. This page explains, in plain language, how Feast handles information.

What Feast does

  • Feast does not require a Feast account.
  • Your saved places and notes are stored on your device.
  • If you use Apple’s iCloud features, your data may sync through your personal iCloud account.
  • If you choose to share a city, that shared content is available to the people you invite through Apple’s sharing tools.

Maps and place data

  • Feast uses Apple Maps to help you search for and save places.
  • Feast does not sell your data.
  • Feast does not use third-party advertising trackers.
  • Feast does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Support

If you contact support, your email will only be used to respond to your message.
