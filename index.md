## Privacy

Feast does not require a Feast account.

Your saved places and notes are stored on your device and may sync through your personal iCloud account when Apple’s iCloud features are enabled. If you choose to share a city, that shared content is available to the people you invite through Apple’s sharing tools.

Feast does not sell your data. Feast does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Feast uses Apple Maps to help you search for and save places. If you contact support, we will only use your email to respond to your request.
